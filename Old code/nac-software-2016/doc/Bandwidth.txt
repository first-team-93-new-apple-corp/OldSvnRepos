Bandwidth (bytes per second)

How To Find: 
1) Open Wireshark

2) Click "Local Area Connection" and press "Start"

3) Stop after 5 seconds

4) Go to "Statistics" and click "Conversations" 

5) Look for the line that has Address A as "DellInc_d8:70:ae" and 
Address B as "National_17:98:42"

6) Column labeled as "bps A<-B" will be the amount of bytes per second 
the robot code is using
